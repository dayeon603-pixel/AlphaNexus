### Results / Observations
- CI nesting invariant (ci95 ⊇ ci68 ⊇ {predicted}) held for all three core timeframes
  with both bull and bear regimes. This is a structural guarantee from the GARCH term
  structure (σ68 = σ, σ95 = 1.96σ).
- Longer horizon → wider CI width confirmed via 1 DAY vs 1 MONTH comparison. GARCH
  cumulative variance scales with horizon steps, not just per-step vol.
- Bull regime correctly produces BUY (mockSignal = clamp((0.75...- 0.5) × 2, -1, 1) ≈
  +0.5 → positive → BUY). Bear regime produces SELL (bullProb=0.25, mockSignal ≈ -0.5).
